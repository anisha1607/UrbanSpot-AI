How It Works

This system uses a multi-agent architecture to analyze NYC data and recommend optimal business locations:

1
Planner Agent: Interprets your input and creates an analysis plan
2
Data Collector Agent: Fetches real-time data from NYC Open Data, Census, and MTA APIs
3
EDA Agent: Performs exploratory analysis, computes scores, and generates visualizations
4
Hypothesis Agent: Produces data-backed recommendations with evidence
5
Critic Agent: Reviews the recommendation and suggests refinements

The system analyzes:

🏪 Competition: Number of existing businesses
💰 Income: Median household income
👥 Population: Demand proxy
🚇 Foot Traffic: Subway ridership data
💸 Rent: Cost indicators

All data is retrieved at runtime - no hardcoded datasets!
